ECE Student Toolkit

Version 1.0 — A comprehensive academic companion designed specifically for Electronics and Communication Engineering students.

Purpose

The ECE Student Toolkit aims to provide a centralized platform where ECE students can access all essential academic tools, study materials, and resources without switching between multiple applications.

Features

  • CGPA, Electronics & Scientific Calculators
  • Engineering Unit Converter
  • Organized Electronics Notes
  • Categorized Formula Sheets
  • Previous Exam Papers Repository
  • Light/Dark Theme Support
